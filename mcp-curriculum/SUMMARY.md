## 🎯 What You Will Learn
The MCP curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive learning experience for AI developers, covering the fundamentals and advanced concepts of Model Context Protocol (MCP). This curriculum is essential for AI developers today, as it enables them to build more efficient and effective AI models. With MCP, developers can create more sophisticated AI systems that can understand and respond to complex contexts. The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, from the introduction to MCP and its architecture to advanced patterns and integrations with other tools and technologies.

The MCP curriculum is divided into eight modules, each covering a specific aspect of MCP. The modules are designed to be self-contained, allowing learners to focus on the topics that interest them the most. However, it is recommended that learners complete the modules in sequence, as each module builds on the previous one. By the end of the curriculum, learners will have a deep understanding of MCP and its applications, as well as the skills to integrate MCP with other tools and technologies.

The MCP curriculum is not just about learning a new technology; it's about gaining a new perspective on AI development. It's about understanding how to create AI systems that can understand and respond to complex contexts, and how to integrate these systems with other tools and technologies. The curriculum is designed to be hands-on, with plenty of examples and exercises to help learners practice their skills. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced AI developer, the MCP curriculum has something to offer.

The importance of MCP cannot be overstated. As AI systems become more complex and sophisticated, the need for a protocol that can handle complex contexts becomes more pressing. MCP is designed to meet this need, providing a flexible and scalable framework for building AI systems that can understand and respond to complex contexts. By learning MCP, developers can gain a competitive edge in the AI market, creating more efficient and effective AI systems that can solve real-world problems.

## 📚 Curriculum Structure

| # | Topic | Description | Est. Time |
|---|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | [Introduction to MCP](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/01-introduction-to-mcp/course.md) | Explain what Model Context Protocol (MCP) is, why it was created, and what it can do | ~45 mins |
| 2 | [MCP Architecture](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/02-mcp-architecture/course.md) | Explain the core architecture of MCP in detail, including a detailed architecture diagram | ~45 mins |
| 3 | [MCP Servers](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/03-mcp-servers/course.md) | Deep dive into MCP servers, including a table comparing what MCP servers can do | ~45 mins |
| 4 | [MCP Tools and Resources](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/04-mcp-tools-and-resources/course.md) | Explain MCP Tools and Resources in depth, including a detailed comparison of MCP tools and resources | ~45 mins |
| 5 | [MCP Clients](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/05-mcp-clients/course.md) | Explain MCP clients in detail, including a table comparing different MCP clients | ~45 mins |
| 6 | [Integrating MCP with LangChain](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/06-mcp-with-langchain/course.md) | Explain how to integrate MCP servers with LangChain, including a table of integration options | ~45 mins |
| 7 | [Integrating MCP with LangGraph](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/07-mcp-with-langgraph/course.md) | Explain how LangGraph nodes call MCP tools, including an ASCII diagram of the integration | ~45 mins |
| 8 | [Advanced MCP Patterns](https://github.com/Shouryaaagarwal/AgenticU/blob/main/mcp-curriculum/08-advanced-mcp-patterns/course.md) | Cover advanced MCP patterns, including a table of all advanced patterns | ~45 mins |

## 🗺️ How to Use This Curriculum

1. Start by completing the introductory module, which explains the basics of MCP and its architecture.
2. Move on to the modules that cover MCP servers, tools, and resources, which provide a deeper understanding of MCP and its applications.
3. Complete the modules that cover integration with other tools and technologies, such as LangChain and LangGraph.
4. Finally, complete the advanced modules, which cover advanced MCP patterns and provide a deeper understanding of MCP and its applications.
5. Practice your skills by completing the exercises and projects provided in each module.
6. Join the MCP community to connect with other learners and get support and feedback on your projects.
7. Take your time and don't be afraid to ask for help if you get stuck.
8. Review and practice regularly to reinforce your learning.
